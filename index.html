How It Works

From zero to employed in three steps

No prior experience needed. Just pick a role and start working.

STEP 01

Choose your job path

Pick the role you want to simulate. Each path comes with its own company, manager, and tech stack.

STEP 02

Complete your weekly tasks

You get 3 real tasks per week — SQL queries, Python scripts, Excel reports. The same work a junior would do at an actual company.

STEP 03

Get AI feedback

Your AI manager (powered by Claude) reviews your work and gives you direct, honest, actionable feedback — just like a real senior colleague would.
